Who We Are

Your Trusted Kerala Travel Partner

Green Gold Vacations was founded in 2005 with a simple belief: that Kerala, with all its natural splendour and cultural depth, deserves to be shared — not as a tourist product, but as a living experience.

Based in Ernakulam, Kerala, our team of passionate local travel experts has spent nearly two decades curating journeys that go beyond the usual highlights. We know the quiet canals that don't appear on maps, the family-run homestays where you'll eat the best Kerala meals of your life, and the festivals that transform a village overnight.

Over 5,000 happy travellers from across India and the world have trusted us with their Kerala dream — and we don't take that lightly.
