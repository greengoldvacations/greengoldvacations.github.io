Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

Simply fill in the enquiry form above or call us directly. Once we understand your requirements we'll share a detailed itinerary and quote within 24 hours.

Can you customise an itinerary for my group?

Absolutely! Every package we offer can be personalised. We specialise in custom itineraries for families, honeymooners, corporate groups, and solo travellers.

What is the best time to visit Kerala?

October to March is ideal — pleasant weather, clear skies, and all attractions open. Monsoon (June–September) has its own lush magic, especially for Ayurveda retreats.

Are all packages inclusive of accommodation and meals?

Package pricing typically includes accommodation, meals as specified, and transport. All inclusions and exclusions are listed clearly in your personalised quote.
