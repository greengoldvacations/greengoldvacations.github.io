Iconic Places to Visit

Each destination tells its own story. Let us help you find yours.

Backwaters

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the "Venice of the East", Alleppey is famous for its tranquil backwater canals, rice paddy fields, and iconic houseboat cruises on Vembanad Lake.

🛶 Houseboat 🌾 Rice Fields 🚣 Canoeing 🎨 Nehru Trophy
Hill Station

Munnar

Perched at 1,600m in the Western Ghats, Munnar is a breathtaking canvas of rolling tea estates, misty peaks, and rare Neelakurinji blooms every 12 years.

🍵 Tea Estates 🏔 Trekking 🦋 Eravikulam Park ❄️ Cool Climate
Wildlife

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar Tiger Reserve, Thekkady offers thrilling wildlife safaris, boat rides on Periyar Lake, and lush spice plantations in the surrounding Kumily town.

🐘 Elephant Safari 🌶 Spice Gardens ⛵ Lake Cruise 🐯 Tiger Reserve
Nature

Wayanad

A green paradise nestled in the highlands of north Kerala, Wayanad is famed for its ancient Edakkal Caves, Soochipara Falls, tribal heritage, and fragrant coffee estates.

🌊 Waterfalls 🪨 Edakkal Caves ☕ Coffee Estates 🌲 Wildlife
Heritage

Fort Kochi

A living museum of Kerala's colonial past, Fort Kochi blends Portuguese, Dutch, British, and Jewish heritage in a charming waterfront setting known for its iconic Chinese fishing nets.

🎣 Chinese Nets 🕍 Jewish Synagogue 🎭 Kathakali 🎨 Biennale
Beach

Varkala

Varkala's dramatic red cliffs, golden sands, and mineral springs make it Kerala's most unique beach destination, paired with Ayurvedic wellness centres and spectacular sunsets.

🏖 Cliff Beach 🧘 Ayurveda 🌅 Sunsets 🌊 Surfing
Beach

Kovalam

Kerala's most famous beach resort town, Kovalam features three crescent beaches lapped by gentle waves, dotted with Ayurvedic resorts and seafood shacks.

🏄 Beach 🍜 Seafood 🧴 Ayurveda 🌊 Swimming
History

Palakkad

Gateway to Kerala through the Palakkad Gap, this city holds the magnificent Tipu Sultan's Fort, Silent Valley National Park, and the scenic Malampuzha Gardens & Reservoir.

🏰 Tipu's Fort 🌿 Silent Valley 🌊 Malampuzha 🚂 Parambikulam
Culture

Thrissur

The cultural capital of Kerala, Thrissur is home to the spectacular Thrissur Pooram elephant festival, ancient temples, classical arts, and the Kerala State Museum.

🐘 Pooram Festival 🎭 Kerala Sangeetha 🛕 Vadakkunnathan 🎨 Natana Kairali
