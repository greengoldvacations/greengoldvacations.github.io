Where We Take You

Each destination tells its own story. Let us help you find yours.

Backwaters

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the "Venice of the East", Alleppey is famous for its tranquil backwater canals, rice paddy fields, and iconic houseboat cruises on Vembanad Lake.

🛶 Houseboat 🌾 Rice Fields 🚣 Canoeing 🎨 Nehru Trophy
Hill Station

Munnar

Perched at 1,600m in the Western Ghats, Munnar is a breathtaking canvas of rolling tea estates, misty peaks, and rare Neelakurinji blooms every 12 years.

🍵 Tea Estates 🏔 Trekking 🦋 Eravikulam Park ❄️ Cool Climate
Wildlife

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar Tiger Reserve, Thekkady offers thrilling wildlife safaris, boat rides on Periyar Lake, and lush spice plantations in the surrounding Kumily town.

🐘 Elephant Safari 🌶 Spice Gardens ⛵ Lake Cruise 🐯 Tiger Reserve
Nature

Wayanad

A green paradise nestled in the highlands of north Kerala, Wayanad is famed for its ancient Edakkal Caves, Soochipara Falls, tribal heritage, and fragrant coffee estates.

🌊 Waterfalls 🪨 Edakkal Caves ☕ Coffee Estates 🌲 Wildlife
Beach · Kerala

Kovalam

Kerala's most iconic beach resort — three crescent-shaped beaches with gentle waves, Ayurvedic resorts, fresh seafood, and spectacular sunsets over the Arabian Sea.

🏄 Beach 🍜 Seafood 🧴 Ayurveda 🌊 Swimming
Mountains · India

Manali

Set in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is India's premier mountain escape — snow-capped peaks, adventure sports, ancient temples, and the legendary Rohtang Pass.

🏔 Snow Peaks ⛷ Adventure Sports 🛕 Hadimba Temple 🚵 Rohtang Pass
Islands · India

Lakshadweep

India's only coral archipelago — pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, and world-class snorkelling and scuba diving in untouched coral reefs.

🏝 Coral Islands 🤿 Scuba Diving 🐠 Snorkelling 🌊 Lagoon Cruises
International

Thailand

From Bangkok's golden temples and floating markets to the idyllic islands of Phuket and Krabi — Thailand is a world of contrasts, colour, and unforgettable experiences.

🛕 Temples 🏝 Islands 🍜 Street Food 🐘 Elephant Sanctuaries
International

Sri Lanka

The Pearl of the Indian Ocean — ancient ruins, lush tea highlands, wildlife-rich national parks, and golden beaches, just a short hop from Kerala.

🏰 Sigiriya Rock 🍵 Tea Highlands 🐘 Yala Safari 🏖 Beaches
