Hill Station · Kerala

Munnar

Perched at 1,600m in the Western Ghats, Munnar enchants with rolling tea estates, misty peaks, rare Neelakurinji blooms and the wildlife of Eravikulam National Park.

🍵 Tea Estates🏔 Trekking🦋 Eravikulam Park❄️ Cool Climate
Heritage · Kerala

Fort Kochi

A living museum blending Portuguese, Dutch and British colonial heritage — iconic Chinese fishing nets, spice warehouses, Kathakali performances and vibrant street art.

🎣 Chinese Nets🎭 Kathakali🕍 Mattancherry🎨 Biennale
Wildlife · Kerala

Thekkady

Home to Periyar Tiger Reserve — cruise on Periyar Lake to spot elephants, explore lush spice plantations and enjoy bamboo rafting through pristine jungle.

🐘 Elephant Safari🌶 Spice Gardens⛵ Lake Cruise🐯 Tiger Reserve
Backwaters · Kerala

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

The "Venice of the East" — drift through tranquil canals on a traditional houseboat, glide past rice paddies and fishing villages on the serene Vembanad Lake.

🛶 Houseboat🌾 Rice Fields🚣 Canoeing🎨 Nehru Trophy
Meadows · Kerala

Vagamon

A hidden highland gem straddling Idukki and Kottayam — vast green meadows, pine forests, misty valleys and paragliding make Vagamon Kerala's best-kept secret.

🌿 Green Meadows🪂 Paragliding🌲 Pine Forests🌫 Misty Valleys
Beach · Kerala

Varkala

Dramatic red laterite cliffs, golden sands and mineral springs make Varkala Kerala's most unique beach — paired with Ayurvedic wellness centres and stunning sunsets.

🏖 Cliff Beach🧘 Ayurveda🌅 Sunsets🌊 Surfing
Beach · Kerala

Kovalam

Kerala's iconic crescent beaches — gentle Arabian Sea waves, Ayurvedic resorts, fresh seafood shacks and spectacular lighthouse views make it a perfect coastal escape.

🏄 Beach🍜 Seafood🧴 Ayurveda🌊 Lighthouse
Culture · Kerala

Trivandrum

Kerala's capital city is home to the magnificent Padmanabhaswamy Temple, the Napier Museum, Zoological Park and the gateway to the beautiful Kovalam coast.

🛕 Padmanabhaswamy🏛 Napier Museum🦁 Zoo🏖 Kovalam Gateway
Nature · Kerala

Wayanad

A green paradise in north Kerala's highlands — ancient Edakkal Caves, thundering Soochipara Falls, tribal heritage, wildlife sanctuaries and fragrant coffee estates.

🌊 Waterfalls🪨 Edakkal Caves☕ Coffee Estates🌲 Wildlife

🏝 Coral Islands🤿 Scuba Diving🐠 Snorkelling🌊 Lagoons
